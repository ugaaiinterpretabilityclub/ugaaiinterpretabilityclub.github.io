About Us

We are the Formal Methods and Aritificial Intelligence Interpretability Directed Reading Group (DRG) at UGA. Our aim is to provide an ecosystem for studying formal methods and AI interpretability at UGA collaboratively. We investigate topics such as mechanistic interpretability, ad-hoc interpretability, formalization, type theory, compositionality, mathematical frameworks for AI, and more. We meet once a month to discuss papers from the reading list in a group seminar format.
